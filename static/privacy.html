Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Nexthena ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App, in accordance with Apple App Store requirements.

1. Data Collection

Nexthena does not collect any personal data.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process:

  • Personal information
  • Contact information
  • Account data
  • Usage analytics
  • Identifiers (such as device IDs or IDFA)
  • Location data
  • Crash or diagnostic data linked to users

2. Data Usage

Since no personal data is collected, Nexthena does not use user data for tracking, advertising, analytics, profiling, or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All content created within Nexthena — including notes, whiteboards, and documents — is stored locally on the user’s device only.

The App does not upload, sync, or back up user content to any external server or cloud service.

4. Network Access and Optional Features

The core functionality of Nexthena works entirely offline.

If optional features require network access (such as AI-powered tools):

  • Network usage is initiated explicitly by the user
  • Data is used only to fulfill the requested feature
  • Any data shared is governed by the privacy policy of the relevant third-party service

5. Third-Party Services

Nexthena does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Optional third-party services, if enabled by the user, operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

6. Data Security

Because all data remains on the user’s device, Nexthena does not have access to user content.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices, including operating system updates and local access controls.

7. Children’s Privacy

Nexthena does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect personal data from users of any age.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
